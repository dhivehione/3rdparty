No Hidden Agendas

When we come to power, you will know exactly what policies will be enacted. Everything is public. Everything is voted on. Nothing is decided in backroom meetings that you never hear about until it's already law.

Think of it as the opposite of traditional politics. You won't wake up one day to find a new law you never knew existed. You'll have voted on it.

How It Works

1

Anyone Can Propose

Any member can draft a policy proposal. New laws. Changes to existing laws. Repealing outdated regulations. You're the architect of the nation's future.

2

Public Debate & Refinement

Proposals are discussed openly. Members can suggest amendments. Members collaborate to refine and improve proposals. The best ideas win.

3

Member Vote

Every member votes. Your Merit Score amplifies your vote — but everyone participates. Vote early in the 7-day window for full weight, or catch up later at reduced weight.

4

Passed = Enacted

If it passes, it becomes policy. No parliamentary tricks. No "we'll consider it." The people's vote is the final word.

Different Votes for Different Decisions

✅

Weighted Majority

For yes/no decisions. The "yes" votes win when they have more Merit Score weight than "no." Threshold varies by impact (50%+ for routine, 60-75% for policy, 80-90% for constitutional).

🏆

Ranked-Choice Voting

For elections and multiple options. Rank your preferences. If your top choice loses, your vote transfers to your second choice. No more "wasted votes."

⚖️

Living Legal Code

Existing laws are always open for re-vote. A law that passed 5 years ago can be amended or repealed if the people change their mind.

⏰

Voting Windows

Primary window (days 1-7): 100% vote weight. Extended window (days 8-10): 50% weight. Vote early for maximum impact.

🤖

Future: Your AI Voting Assistant

Too busy to read every proposal? We get it. In the future, you'll be able to train your personal AI agent on your preferences. It will vote on your behalf when you don't have time — matching your known positions.

Coming later: You always retain full control. Override any vote your AI agent makes. Or turn it off entirely. Your choice.
