Accountability & Recall

Yearly Appraisals

All leaders undergo annual performance reviews. Ratings based on community feedback and goal achievement.

Term Limits

Maximum 2-year terms. Leaders must re-apply and be re-elected to serve again.

Recall Process

Members can initiate recall with 10% petition. 80% supermajority vote removes leader.

Decision Statements

All significant decisions documented and published. Public vote required for policies.
