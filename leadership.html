How to Become Important (Process)

1
Join First
We can't make you leader if you're not even in the party. That's just how memberships work.
2
Do Stuff
Vote on proposals, write policies, help out, bring friends. Your merit score is built from actual work, not just existing.
3
Wait for It
We need enough members first. Can't have leadership before we have people to lead. That's just math.
4
Apply & Get Interviewed
Write why you want the job. Then do a live public interview. Yes, your actual face. Yes, everyone can watch. Terrifying? Good.
5
Get Voted In
Members rank candidates. Winner gets the job. If you suck, they can vote you out. That's the "recall" thing.
