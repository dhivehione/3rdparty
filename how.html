How the Merit Score Works

Yes, there's math. The app handles it.

🧠 The Basics

Your Merit Score is built from three things:

  • Skills & Professional Expertise (lawyers, engineers, doctors — what you bring to the table)
  • On‑the‑Ground Action (organizing, cleanups, Círculos, physical work)
  • Ideas & Proposals (policy suggestions that get community support)
  • Donations to the Live Treasury (with diminishing returns so big money can't buy power)

All contributions are verified by the community. No fake points.

⚖️ Why This Exists

📜

Laws Should Be Living

We're often ruled by rules written decades ago for a different world. In the 3d Party, laws are living documents — you can directly propose changes when they no longer make sense. No more being bound by 100‑year‑old decisions you had no say in.

🗳️

Direct Citizen Voting

We will not outsource democracy to parliament. Every member votes directly on legislation, budgets, and rules. No gerrymandering. No middlemen. Your voice counts exactly once.

🎯

The 3% Strategy

We only need 13,000 people (~3% of the population) to build this new system. Every single member is crucial. There are no filler names here — you will know exactly where you stand and what you'll get.

🔍

No Insider Connections

Traditional parties treat members as statistics. Here, your influence is transparent: it's your Merit Score. No backroom deals, no family ties, no secret handshakes. Just what you actually contribute.

So how much does this matter?

The Merit Score opens doors: funding for your proposals, leadership roles, and actual voting weight on some decisions. It's designed so that consistent, genuine contributors rise naturally — and no one can buy their way to the top.

The full algorithm is public and community‑auditable. Read the Whitepaper for the technical details.

When We Form Government – No More Cronyism

When the 3d Party forms government, things will work very differently.

Any qualified member of the movement can apply for Cabinet Minister and other high-level positions (ambassadors, heads of independent institutions, etc.) — exactly like applying for a real job.

No more handing top posts to family, friends, or party loyalists.

The entire selection process will be nationally televised in an entertaining format: live interviews judged by the country's top experts, with public voting — just like a big TV show. The winner is announced live on TV, and the next day the President must accept them into office.

Once appointed, every minister and high official will go through a 3-month probation period and regular annual performance appraisals — exactly like any other professional employee. Underperformance means replacement.
