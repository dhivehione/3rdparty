How It Works

A digital ladder to real power. Here's exactly how you climb it.

1

Join — This Is Where It Starts

Name and phone number. That's it. No complicated forms, no donation required. You're not signing up for a newsletter — you're becoming a co-owner of a movement.

2

Contribute — Your Way

There's no single path. Pick what fits you:

🛠️

Your Skills

Lawyers, engineers, doctors — whatever you bring to the table

🤝

On-the-Ground Action

Organizing, cleanups, community work

💡

Ideas & Proposals

Policy suggestions that get community support

💰

Donations

Support the treasury (no one can buy power)

3

Earn Merit — Transparently

Your Merit Score is your influence in the movement. Not based on money, not based on who you know — based on what you actually contribute.

You earn points by voting on proposals, writing successful policies, bringing in active members, completing tasks on the bounty board, and donating (with steep diminishing returns — $10 gets more influence proportionally than $10,000).

Old contributions decay over time (18-month half-life) so you have to keep participating. Sitting on early membership doesn't grant eternal power.

Higher merit opens doors: funding for your proposals, leadership roles, and voting weight on key decisions. The app handles all the math — you just contribute. Read the Whitepaper for full details.

4

Vote — Directly on Laws

No middlemen. No parliament outsourcing. Every member votes directly on laws, budgets, and rules — down to the specific clause level.

  • Your voice counts exactly once — no gerrymandering
  • Laws are living documents you can change when they stop making sense
  • Support, oppose, or abstain — and explain why publicly
  • Right now: one-person-one-vote. Later: merit-weighted (but your vote still counts)
5

Lead — If You Earn It

Leadership here isn't given. It's applied for, verified, earned, and endorsed by members. Every position — from Governing Council to Cabinet Minister — follows the same rigorous path.

When we form government, any qualified member can apply for any high government post — Cabinet Minister, Ambassador, Independent Commission President, SOE Director, Government SEO Chairperson. Every role is treated like a real job application. No family connections, no backroom deals, no lifetime appointments.

When we form government, the selection process for high posts will be nationally televised: live interviews judged by top experts, with public voting. The winner announced live on air. Imagine watching your neighbour become a Cabinet Minister — because they earned it, not because they knew someone.

Every appointee goes through a 3-month probation and annual performance reviews — just like any professional employee. Term limits: 2 years max. Recallable by member vote. We believe all citizens shall have the honour to serve the nation at some point in their life — as leaders or shapers of history. Service is a duty and an honour, not a career.

🗳️ What Happens When We Win

Our elected candidates are bound to the "People's Manifesto" — the set of policies that members already voted for. If a representative consistently votes against the platform's referendums, we initiate a recall and field a challenger.

It's a digital leash. They're delegates, not rulers.

Why This System?

Because the old way clearly isn't working. The same families, the same faces, the same broken promises every five years. We're not building a better party — we're building a better system.

Merit Score ensures influence comes from contribution, not cash or connections. Direct voting ensures laws reflect what people actually want. Transparent selection ensures leaders are chosen for ability, not ancestry.

This isn't gamification. It's a blueprint for a nation where every citizen can rise — and no one gets to stay on top forever.
