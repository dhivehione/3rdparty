How It Works

A new kind of politics — built by the people, for the people.

🌍 The Problem

Traditional parties are stuck in the past. Laws written decades ago. Decisions made by a few. Power handed to family and friends.

We're building something different — a system where every citizen has real power, and no one can buy influence.

🗳️ Direct Democracy

No middlemen. No parliament outsourcing. Every member votes directly on laws, budgets, and rules.

  • Your voice counts exactly once — no gerrymandering
  • Laws are living documents you can change when they stop making sense
  • Decisions made by the many, not the few

🎯 The 3% Strategy

We only need 13,000 people (~3% of the population) to build this new system. Every single member matters. There are no filler names here — you'll know exactly where you stand and what you'll get.

🧠 The Merit Score

To keep things fair, we track contributions through a Merit Score. Think of it as a transparent way to recognize who's genuinely building the movement.

Higher merit opens doors: funding for your proposals, leadership roles, and voting weight on key decisions. But don't worry — the app handles all the math. You just contribute.

Designed so consistent contributors rise naturally. No one can buy their way to the top. Read the Whitepaper for full details.

🏛️ When We Form Government

No more cronyism. Any qualified member can apply for Cabinet Minister and other high-level positions — exactly like applying for a real job.

The selection process will be nationally televised: live interviews judged by top experts, with public voting. The winner is announced live on TV.

Every minister goes through a 3-month probation and annual performance reviews — just like any professional employee.
