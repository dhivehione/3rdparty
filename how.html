So what actually is the 3d Party?

We're glad you asked. (Most people just assume we're confused.)

The Setup

For too long, Maldives politics has been a two-team game. Pick a side, chant the chants, repeat the slogans. If you're tired of choosing between Team A and Team A-Plus, you basically had no home.

That's... not great for a healthy democracy. But here we are.

The Answer

The 3d Party is the actual third option. Not "third party" as in insurance (we can't stress this enough), but third as in: here's a different way to do politics.

No backroom deals. No dynasty building. No "my family has been in politics for 40 years so respect me."

How We Do It: Merit Score

Here's the deal: we don't decide leadership based on who you know, how much money you have, or how long your family has been in the game.

"Your influence is earned, not inherited."

— Actually, that's just how it works here.

What Gets You Points?

  • 🧠
    Skills & Expertise — Lawyers, designers, teachers, engineers. People who actually know things.
  • 🏃
    On-the-Ground Action — Events, cleanups, organizing. The people who show up and do the work.
  • 💡
    Ideas & Proposals — Actual policy suggestions. Real bridge-building, not the metaphorical kind.
  • 💰
    Support & Donations — Money helps, but here's the thing: we have diminishing returns. Big donations get you less score percentage than small ones. Money can't buy the movement.

Why This Matters

Traditional politics runs on connections. Your dad knows someone, your family has history, you went to school with the right people.

The 3d Party runs on what you actually do. Skills, effort, ideas, work. That's it. No secret handshakes required.

(We know this sounds revolutionary. It's really just... fair.)
