How the Live Treasury Works

1. Donation Submitted

Members submit proof of donation with a deposit slip. Donor identity is verified internally; a nickname appears publicly.

2. Admin Verified

An admin cross-checks the deposit slip. Once verified, the donation appears instantly in this public ledger.

3. Expenditures Logged

Every expense — operational costs, Círculos budgets, media funding — is recorded here by admins after public vote approval.
